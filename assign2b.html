Wildlife Specials

Royal Bengal Tiger

The national animal of India, primarily found in the Sundarbans and Central Indian forests. It symbolizes the power and conservation of our wild habitats.

Asiatic Lion

Found exclusively in the Gir Forest of Gujarat, these lions are a unique part of India's heritage and the only lions found in the wild outside Africa.

Snow Leopard

Known as the "Ghost of the Mountains," these elusive cats inhabit the rugged, high-altitude terrain of the Himalayan mountain ranges.

One-Horned Rhino

This armored giant is a major conservation success story, residing in the marshy grasslands of Kaziranga National Park in Assam.

Asian Elephant

A National Heritage Animal of India, playing a vital role in forest ecosystems through seed dispersal and forest path creation.

Indian Peacock

The National Bird of India, celebrated for its vibrant blue plumage and spectacular display during the monsoon rains.
