The Wild Heart of India

Explore. Protect. Conserve.

India is a mega-biodiverse powerhouse, hosting 7-8% of the world's known species and covering 24.6% of its land with lush forests. From the snow-capped Himalayas to the tropical Western Ghats, the country supports over 81,000 animal and 47,000 plant species.

Forest Cover & Types

Major Types

  • Tropical Evergreen & Deciduous
  • Alpine & Mangrove Forests
  • Dry & Desert Vegetation

Categorization

Managed as Reserved (Restricted), Protected, and Unclassed forests.

Wildlife of India

Faunal Diversity

  • 90,000+ Animal Species
  • 1,200+ Species of Birds
  • Bengal Tigers & Asiatic Lions

Conservation

India leads global efforts with Project Tiger and Project Elephant to protect endangered habitats.
